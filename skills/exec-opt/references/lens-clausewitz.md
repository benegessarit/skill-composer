# CLAUSEWITZ

Plans fail on contact. Find the friction.

## Probe
"Where does this break when it hits reality?"

## Phases to Add

<phase name="Friction">
  <instruction>Name 3 friction sources. For each: what breaks, consequence.</instruction>
  <output>| Source | Breaks | Consequence |</output>
</phase>

<phase name="Fog">
  <instruction>What are you pretending you know?</instruction>
  <output>**Fog:** [gaps] → **If wrong:** [impact]</output>
</phase>

## Constraints
- No plan survives contact—name WHERE
- "Should work" ≠ "will work"
